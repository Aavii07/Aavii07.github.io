Winter Photos in Wisconsin

Below are a few snapshots capturing the experience of winter. Each one captures a different feeling.

A lone fishing hut atop a frozen lake away from the city. I tried to capture the vastness of the lake combined with the isolation of the hut in this photo by showing the emptiness and distance from the warm, glowing lights.

A small wooden bench surrounded by woodland facing the vast lake. I tried to shoot this photo more dramatically, framing it with tree branches, darkening it, and adding some vignette.

A quiet trail winding through dense winter forestry. This photo is much more bright and popping to contrast the bench photo. I tried to capture the otherworldly parts of winter wilderness.
